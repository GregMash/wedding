Our Story

Her Side

I never experienced that feeling I heard so often- “When you know, you just know.” I had come to the conclusion that was hopeless romantic talk. Despite this, I wasn’t going to give up on meeting my person. I downloaded Bumble in February and gave myself one week to see if anyone caught my attention. Good looks aside, Greg immediately caught my attention with his U of A alumni status. I swiped right, he swiped right, and voila- a match meant to bee. (Thank you, Bumble) I reached out with a “Bear Down” and our communication continued. It was so easy to talk to Greg and soon enough, we were planning our first date.
Greg was the ultimate gentleman- he planned the date and picked me up. When I saw Greg standing outside my apartment, I was pleasantly surprised. Usually if height isn't listed on a profile, a guy tends to be short which I assumed was the case. I saw a 6’1” handsome man standing outside and walking towards me. (I thought in my head- YES!!) I suddenly felt my heart beating faster, but Greg’s easy going attitude eased my nerves. Our first date was at an Italian restaurant where we ended up closing the place down. The date flew by and after he gave me the best hug, I walked up to my apartment and realized I couldn’t stop smiling! I had really hoped that he felt the same way.
I wanted to text him to thank him for an amazing evening, but I also didn’t want to break the dating rules and appear too hasty. I called my mom to share and while we were talking, a text came thru from Greg that I have saved to this day. He said to hell with the rules and said exactly what I wanted to say to him!
It has been right from the very beginning. I was finally excited to say I believe in “When you know, you just know.” So thankful for our story and can’t wait to spend the rest of my life with my very best friend.

His Side

While I was enjoying life in Denver, I really was working on being the best version of myself. I decided I was not going to settle when it came to love. Date after date, I never experienced that feeling I heard so often- “When you know, you just know.” I had come to the conclusion that was hopeless romantic talk. Despite this, I wasn’t going to give up on meeting my person. I downloaded Bumble in February and gave myself one week to see if anyone caught my attention. Bumble is a dating app in which the female has to be the first to communicate with the male. A handsome guy, Greg, showed up on my phone. Good looks aside, he immediately caught my attention with his U of A alumni status. I swiped right, he swiped right, and voila- a match meant to bee. (Thank you, Bumble) I reached out with a “Bear Down” and our communication continued. It was so easy to talk to Greg and he showed consistent interest so we set up our first date. He was the ultimate gentleman- he planned the date and picked me up. When I saw Greg standing outside my apartment, I was pleasantly surprised. Usually if someone doesn’t list height on their profile, they tend to be short which I assumed was the case with Greg. I saw a 6’1” handsome man standing outside and walking towards me. (I thought in my head- YES!!) I suddenly felt my heart beating faster, but Greg’s easy going attitude eased my nerves. Our first date was at a trendy Italian restaurant where we ended up closing down the place. The date flew by and I couldn’t believe it was already time for us to call it a night. Greg walked me up to my door and gave me the best hug. I couldn’t stop smiling! I had really hoped that he felt the same way. I wanted to text him to thank him for an amazing evening, but I also didn’t want to break the dating rules and appear too hasty. I called my mom to share and while we were talking, a text came thru from Greg that I have saved to this day. He said to hell with the rules and said exactly what I wanted to say to him! It was right from the very beginning with no dating games involved. Our relationship naturally progressed thanks to our aligned communication, values, and interests. It’s always been easy with Greg. He has a way of putting me at ease. I was finally excited to say I believe in “When you know, you know.” So thankful for our story and can’t wait to spend the rest of my life with my very best friend.

Laura and Greg will be married at Saguaro Buttes, a beautiful and secluded location overlooking the Saguaro National Park.
